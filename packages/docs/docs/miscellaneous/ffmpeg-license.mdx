Remotion distributes a compiled FFmpeg binary under the [GPLv2+ license](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/gpl-2.0.html). The [source and build scripts](https://github.com/remotion-dev/rust-ffmpeg-splitter) are publicly available.

## x264 and x265

x264 and x265 are licensed under the GPL, not the LGPL. Therefore, Remotion's FFmpeg binary cannot be distributed under the LGPL.

## fdk-aac

Remotion does not use the official fdk-aac distribution. The [build script](https://github.com/remotion-dev/rust-ffmpeg-splitter/blob/main/compile-fdkaac.mjs) shows that Remotion's FFmpeg binary uses the free `fdk-aac-free` variant, which has no licensing concerns.

## Other components

No other components in Remotion's FFmpeg binary should raise licensing concerns.

Remotion has carefully verified the licensing information on this page. Concerns can be sent to [hi@remotion.dev](mailto:hi@remotion.dev).
